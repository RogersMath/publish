# The Authorship Shift: Writing for the World, Not the Algorithm

AI has eliminated the scarcity of text. The bottleneck in writing is no longer creation—it is judgment, clarity, voice, and reputation.

This site argues for a fundamental shift in English education:  
**from private essays to public authorship.**

---

## <span id="why"></span>Why This Shift Matters

Traditional essays are private performances.  
Public writing is real communication.

Students learn more when they write for people rather than for rubrics.

---

## <span id="authorship"></span>Authorship as the New Literacy

With AI assisting drafting, students must learn how to:

- refine ideas  
- edit collaboratively  
- develop authentic voice  
- create for real audiences  
- publish responsibly  

Publishing, not producing text, is the real 21st-century literacy.

## <span id="faculty"></span>For Faculty: Why This Improves Teaching

- plagiarism concerns disappear  
- revision becomes meaningful  
- assessment becomes authentic  
- engagement increases  
- writing gains purpose  

Faculty become mentors of clarity, not enforcers of compliance.
